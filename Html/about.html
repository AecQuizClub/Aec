Our History

1964

On 30th July, the first Quiz was conducted by
Daniel L Miller at Cotton College which was won by
Karunabhiram Rajkhowa, a student of AEC.

2008

The first quiz blog of North East was created.

2009

We came through as the
Runners up at the BRAIN JAM QUIZ.

2010

We went one step further and team,
comprising Mharnav Gogoi and Parikshit Pegu
bagged the First prize at the BRAIN JAM QUIZ.
A package trip to Singapore
was sponsored for them by Sona bath fittings.

2011:

AECians represented Generation Awakens,
an NGO, where we worked as content
co-ordinators(after completing graduation).
We won an all expense paid trip to
Hotel Taj Mahal Palace- Mumbai.

2008

Maharnav Gogoi, Amrit Pritom Chetia,
and Bidyut Bailung, students of AEC, reached the
National
Finals of IQ 2008- India's biggest
Undergraduate Inter- College Quiz,
held in Singapore, and
secured the second position

2012

Kapinjal Chowdhury and Kamanasish
Chowdhury represented AEC at
the Rajagiri National Business
Quiz, 2012, and took the
championship title at the Zonal Round.

2016

AEC Quiz Club inducted into
Student Activity Centre with
Sumantha Sarathi Borthakur as
President and Dhiman Kakati as
Secretary. Logo designed by
Gautam Sarmah.

2017

Prof Biswanath Dekaraja takes
over as Faculty in charge

2012

The first of its kind in Assam-a
7 day quiz marathon was conducted by AECians.
